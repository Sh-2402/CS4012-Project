Jaipur, India

Jaipur, India

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, India, is known as the "Pink City" due to its distinct pink-colored buildings in the old city area. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture.

Culture

Jaipur, the vibrant Pink City of India, captivates visitors with its rich blend of royal heritage and lively culture. From colorful festivals, mesmerizing folk dances, and artisanal crafts to mouthwatering cuisine and grand palaces, Jaipur offers a unique and unforgettable experience. Renowned for its warm hospitality and tradition of welcoming guests like family, Jaipur invites every traveler to discover its charm, beauty, and timeless spirit—making it a must-visit destination for anyone seeking culture, history, and adventure.

Things to Do

Amber Fort

Majestic hilltop fortress with stunning architecture, elephant rides, and captivating light & sound show revealing its royal history.

Hawa Mahal

Iconic Palace of Winds with 953 lattice windows allowing royal women to observe street life. Best at sunrise or sunset.

City Palace

Royal complex with museums, courtyards, and gardens showcasing Jaipur's rich heritage and former royal residence.

Jal Mahal

Serene Water Palace in Man Sagar Lake offering picturesque views, best enjoyed from the lakeside.

Jantar Mantar

UNESCO-listed astronomical observatory featuring world's largest sundial and unique celestial tracking instruments.

Galta Ji

Beautiful Monkey Temple complex in natural gorge with sacred water tanks and lively monkey population.
